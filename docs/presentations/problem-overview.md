## Short Description (for submissions, social, or video description)

Prediction markets are growing fast, but they still can't serve institutions or knowledge economies. X-Market identifies three structural gaps: binary Yes/No markets can't express continuous probability; paid signal sellers lack verifiable on-chain track records; and trading vs. insights settle on separate data sources with no single event truth. The fix is to treat events as probability distributions — not fragmented outcome bets — and unify trading and paid knowledge on one EventRoot with shared Oracle settlement on Sui.
